# rjapplin_dissertation
This contains all the code necessary to produce the results in *Applin (2025).*  Although post-processed result sets that power certain figures and tables are provided, raw data is not and cannot be provided. Chapter 1 uses data purchased from Inside Airbnb thanks to generous funding from the Economics Department at the University of Kentucky. Chapters 2 and 3 use data from NielsenIQ Datasets at the Kilts Center for Marketing Data Center at The University of Chicago Booth School of Business and require an active subscription to access.

**Bibliography**

R. Jacob Applin, *Applied Essays in Industrial Organization: Pricing in Short-Term Rental Markets and Policy Implications in Tobacco Consumption* (PhD diss., University of Kentucky, 2025).
